Thesis

Aritzia is undervalued by ~40% on a SOTP basis. Brand momentum, US store rollout, and operating leverage drive multi-year compounding.

Two conditions: First, US revenue continues compounding above 30% as the store rollout scales. Second, operating margins expand toward 18% as the ecommerce mix grows and the supply chain stabilizes.

If both hold, Aritzia compounds at 25%+ over three years from a margin of safety near 30%.

Kill Line

Three conditions close the position. Same-store sales turn negative for two consecutive quarters. US expansion stalls below 8 new stores per year. Operating margin contracts below 12% on a trailing-twelve-months basis.

Any one invalidates the multi-year compounding thesis. Position closes within ten trading days.
